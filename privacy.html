Privacy Policy

At TRUST-BD, we are committed to protecting your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data while using our Bangladeshi investment platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your full name and email address
  • National ID or verification documents (if applicable)
  • Investment history, transaction records
  • IP address, device data, and session activity

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, verify, and manage your TRUST-BD investment account
  • To ensure compliance with financial regulations in Bangladesh
  • To send investment updates, notifications, and offers
  • To enhance platform security and user experience

3. Data Security

We use advanced security measures including end-to-end encryption, secure database storage, and multi-level access control to keep your data safe and confidential.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share limited data with regulators or legal authorities only when required by law of Bangladesh.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request data deletion or correction
  • You can opt out of marketing communications
  • You can view your full account history at any time

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact our support team via the app or website support section.
